---
title: "Explain It to a Child"
number: 5
keywords: ["simplicity", "clarity", "communication"]
asset_url: ""
---

Explain your idea using only words a ten-year-old would know. No jargon, no acronyms, no hand-waving. If you cannot make it simple, you do not yet understand it well enough. The struggle to simplify is the struggle to think clearly.
