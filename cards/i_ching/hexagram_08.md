Water over earth, flowing together naturally. This hexagram speaks of forming bonds and alliances. Seek those whose values align with yours and commit to mutual support. But examine your own sincerity first. Lasting unity requires genuine goodwill, not mere convenience. Are you prepared to show up consistently?
