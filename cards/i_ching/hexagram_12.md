Heaven and earth draw apart, communication ceases. This is a time of stagnation where efforts meet resistance and connections falter. Do not force progress. The wise person withdraws inward, preserves their values, and waits for conditions to shift. Stagnation is temporary. Maintain your integrity through the drought.
