Thunder erupts from the earth and music begins. Enthusiasm moves people to action. This is the time to rally support, launch projects, and ride the wave of shared excitement. But enthusiasm must be grounded in real substance. Inspire others through authentic conviction, not empty spectacle. Move with the energy while it flows.
