Fire at the foot of the mountain, illuminating its shape. Grace gives form to substance. Attend to how things appear, not just how they function. Clear expression, elegant design, and beautiful presentation serve the truth when they clarify rather than obscure. But remember that grace adorns content; it does not replace it.
