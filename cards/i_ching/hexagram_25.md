Thunder under heaven, all things moving in accordance with their nature. Act from genuine impulse rather than calculated strategy. This hexagram rewards sincerity and punishes artifice. When unexpected events arise, respond naturally and without overthinking. Plans built on manipulation will fail. Plans built on honest intent will find unexpected support.
