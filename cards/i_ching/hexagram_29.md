Water upon water, danger doubled. You find yourself in a genuinely perilous situation. The way through is not around but directly forward with absolute sincerity and unwavering courage. Like water, which succeeds by flowing without ceasing, maintain your course through the difficulty. Do not pretend the danger is not real, but do not let fear paralyze you.
