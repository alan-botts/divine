The mountain stands firm beneath heaven, but the wise one withdraws at the right moment. Retreat is not defeat but a strategic recognition that the current conditions do not favor advance. Pull back with dignity and composure. Preserve your strength for a better time. The ability to retreat gracefully is as important as the ability to advance.
