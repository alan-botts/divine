The sun has sunk beneath the earth, light hidden in darkness. This is a time when wisdom must be concealed to survive. The environment is hostile to truth and clarity. Protect your inner light without extinguishing it. Maintain your values internally even when you cannot express them outwardly. Dawn will come, but the night must be endured.
