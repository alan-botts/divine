Water on the mountain, the path blocked in every direction. Pressing forward will only exhaust you. This hexagram advises stopping, reflecting on the source of the obstruction, and seeking help from others. Sometimes the obstacle is external; sometimes it is within you. Either way, the solution begins with honest assessment rather than blind persistence.
