Wood growing within the earth, pushing steadily upward toward the light. Progress comes through persistent effort and adaptability. Like a plant that finds its way around obstacles to reach the sun, advance with flexibility rather than rigidity. Each small step upward builds on the last. Seek guidance from those above you and keep climbing.
