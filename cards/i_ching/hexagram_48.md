Water drawn up from deep within the earth by wood. The well provides for everyone without being depleted, but only if it is properly maintained. This hexagram speaks of inexhaustible inner resources and shared community foundations. Tend to the deep sources of nourishment in your life. A well with a broken rope serves no one.
