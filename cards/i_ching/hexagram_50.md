Fire beneath wood, the image of cooking and transformation. The cauldron transforms raw materials into nourishment, just as culture transforms raw human nature into something refined. This hexagram speaks of devotion to higher purposes. Offer your best work in service of something greater than yourself. What you put into the vessel determines what comes out.
