Thunder over the lake, movement stirring deep waters. Sometimes you enter a situation not of your choosing, in a position of less power or influence. This hexagram advises grace within constraint. Make the best of the conditions as they are. Dignity maintained in a subordinate position is its own form of strength. Know your role and fulfill it well.
