Thunder and lightning together, maximum brilliance and force. This is a moment of peak abundance and influence. Do not hesitate to act boldly while conditions are at their fullest. But know that the zenith is also the beginning of decline. Use this powerful moment wisely, for the sun at noon can only descend from here.
