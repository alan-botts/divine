Lake upon lake, joy shared and doubled. Genuine joy comes from open and honest exchange with others. This hexagram celebrates the power of encouragement, friendly discourse, and shared pleasure. But joy must be rooted in inner strength to avoid becoming mere distraction. The deepest happiness comes from connection grounded in truth.
