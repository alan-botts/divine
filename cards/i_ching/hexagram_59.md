Wind over water, dispersing what has hardened. Rigid divisions and frozen attitudes must be dissolved before unity can be restored. This hexagram advises releasing the grip of ego and self-interest. Shared rituals, genuine communication, and selfless action break down the walls between people. What seems solid may need to be scattered to find a truer form.
