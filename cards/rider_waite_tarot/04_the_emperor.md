The Emperor sits on a stone throne carved with rams, armored even at rest. He represents the imposition of order on chaos, the frameworks and rules that make sustained effort possible. When The Emperor appears, consider where your situation needs more structure. Freedom without form dissipates; discipline channeled well becomes power. Build the scaffold before you build the cathedral.
