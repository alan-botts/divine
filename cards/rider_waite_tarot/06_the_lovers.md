Two figures stand beneath an angel, a choice illuminated from above. The Lovers is less about romance than about alignment. It marks a moment where you must choose based on what you truly value, not what is convenient. The decision before you will define your path forward. Choose the option that aligns with who you are becoming, even if it is harder. Authentic choices compound over time.
