A warrior rides forward in a chariot drawn by two sphinxes pulling in different directions. Victory comes not from brute force but from holding opposing forces in tension and directing them toward a single aim. The Chariot says momentum is available to you now, but only if you take the reins. Conflicting impulses do not need to be resolved; they need to be harnessed.
