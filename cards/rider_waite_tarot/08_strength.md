A woman gently closes the jaws of a lion, not through force but through calm presence. Strength here is not about overpowering but about endurance, patience, and the quiet courage to face what frightens you without flinching. When this card appears, the challenge before you will not be won by aggression. Meet it with steady hands and an open heart. Gentleness is its own kind of power.
