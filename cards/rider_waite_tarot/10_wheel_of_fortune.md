A great wheel turns, figures rising on one side and falling on the other. The Wheel of Fortune marks a shift in circumstances beyond your control. What was stuck begins to move; what was stable begins to change. This card reminds you that all conditions are temporary. Rather than clinging to the current state, prepare to adapt. The wheel turns for everyone, and your position on it is about to change.
