A skeletal rider moves across the field; none are spared, but behind the figure the sun rises on a new horizon. Death rarely means literal ending. It signals that a chapter is closing and something must be released before the next one can begin. Clinging to what has already ended only prolongs discomfort. Let the old form fall away. What replaces it will be more alive than what you are holding onto.
