A figure kneels by a pool beneath a sky of bright stars, pouring water onto the land and back into the stream. After the destruction of The Tower comes The Star, offering calm restoration and renewed purpose. This card is a sign that healing is underway. Trust the process. Inspiration is returning, and the path ahead, though not yet fully visible, is opening. Let yourself be guided by what gives you genuine hope.
