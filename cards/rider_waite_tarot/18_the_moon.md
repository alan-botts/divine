A moon looks down on a winding path between two towers, a dog and a wolf howling at its light, a crayfish emerging from the water. The Moon illuminates just enough to create shadows. This card warns that things are not as they appear. Anxiety and confusion may be distorting your perception. Do not make permanent decisions based on temporary fears. Wait for better light before committing to a direction.
