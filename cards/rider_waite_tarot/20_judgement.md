An angel sounds a trumpet and figures rise from their graves, arms outstretched. Judgement is the moment of reckoning where you evaluate your life honestly and answer a call to something higher. This card signals that a major decision or realization is at hand. You are being asked to rise to the occasion, to shed old identities and step into the role you were meant to play. Answer the call.
