A dancer floats within a great wreath, the four elements at each corner. The World marks the successful completion of a cycle. What you set out to do has been accomplished, and you stand in a moment of wholeness before the next journey begins. Take time to acknowledge what you have achieved. Integration means carrying forward what you have learned. The end of one cycle is the threshold of another.
