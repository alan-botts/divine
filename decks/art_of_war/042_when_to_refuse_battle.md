---
title: "When to Refuse Battle"
number: 42
keywords: ["restraint", "patience", "discipline"]
asset_url: ""
---

If the situation is unfavorable, refuse to engage -- even if the opponent taunts you. Draw a line on the ground and dare them to cross it, but do not cross theirs. The ability to decline an engagement on unfavorable terms is as important as the ability to win one.
