---
title: "Appear Weak When Strong"
number: 44
keywords: ["deception", "surprise", "concealment"]
asset_url: ""
---

When you are capable, feign incapacity. When you are active, feign inactivity. When you are ready, appear unready. The opponent who underestimates you will overextend. The greatest advantage is the one your competition does not know you have.
