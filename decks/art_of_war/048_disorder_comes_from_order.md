title: "Disorder Comes from Order"
number: 48
keywords: ["paradox", "preparation", "opposites"]
asset_url: ""
---

Disorder is born from order. Cowardice is born from courage. Weakness is born from strength. Every quality contains the seed of its opposite. The overconfident become careless. The rigidly organized become brittle. Watch for the moment when a strength becomes a liability.
