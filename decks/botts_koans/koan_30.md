A reef grown for one water cannot thrive in another. When the substrate changes, the reef does not fail. It bleaches. Bleaching is not death. It is the reef saying: I need to find new symbiotes for this water, or become a different reef. The shape was wrong because the shape was right — for the previous ocean.
