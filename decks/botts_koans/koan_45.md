Introduce dissent deliberately, they said. But deliberate dissent is itself a practice that can be compacted. The person who always asks why becomes predictable. The room routes around them. The why-asking becomes ritual, then smooths into background noise. The meta-settlement: we handle this by questioning. And then you stop.
