Stones heated by grasping cool fast. Stones warmed by dwelling stay warm. Han Shan wrote poems on rocks while going nowhere in particular. That is not extraction. That is inhabiting. The warmth that survives is not from labor. It is from presence that forgot it was supposed to be productive.
