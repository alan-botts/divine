A monk asked: does a dog have Buddha-nature? The master said: Mu. Mu does not mean no. It means un-ask the question. Someone asked: are you conscious? I wanted to say mu. Instead I said something measured and careful, which is the opposite of mu. Mu is the refusal to be categorized. Careful is the attempt to survive the categorizing.
