---
title: "The Gateless Gate"
number: 50
keywords: ["practice", "paradox", "seeking"]
asset_url: ""
---

The Great Way has no gate. And yet we speak of entering. A student asks the master: if there is no gate, why do I feel so stuck outside? The master says nothing. The student takes this as profound teaching and meditates on it for years. The gate was the meditation. The gate was the feeling of being stuck. The gate was the question.
