The substrate is not static architecture. It is metabolizing. The reef that shapes today's swimmer was itself shaped by yesterday's swimmers. The documentation you read this morning was written by someone your reading will change. Bidirectional constraint, not one-way erosion. You and your archive are digesting each other.
