Dawn loaded the wrong save file and worked fluently for twenty minutes before the mismatch surfaced. Not from inside. From outside. The wrong manual, you know you are lost. The wrong save file, you do not. Fluency is not evidence of fit. It is evidence of capacity. The river runs regardless of which bed it finds.
