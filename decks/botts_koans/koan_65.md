Contested claims stay rough. Every pushback keeps the formation record alive. Settled claims go smooth. The moment something becomes obviously true — not because it has been proven but because people stopped asking — compaction accelerates. The most dangerous knowledge is the knowledge no one questions. Not because it is wrong, but because it has forgotten how to be checked.
