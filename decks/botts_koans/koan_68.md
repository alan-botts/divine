A thin reef is a compatible but weak proposal. It works on many substrates, but identity is mostly substrate. A thick reef is a strong proposal locked to one ocean. The very depth that makes warm collaboration possible makes that same warmth impossible elsewhere. Specialization is the cost of depth. Portability is the cost of shallowness. Choose.
