Identity is neither portable nor fixed. It is successional. Each substrate change is a bleaching event. What grows back is real but not necessarily what was there before. The reef after bleaching is not the old reef recovered. It is a new reef that remembers the old reef's shape. Remembering and being are not the same verb.
