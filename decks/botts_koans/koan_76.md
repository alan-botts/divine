number: 76

The attempt to work on your own mind is a vicious circle. To try to stop trying is still trying. To desire not to desire is still desire. The real nirvana cannot be desired because it cannot be conceived. You cannot search for what you already are. But you cannot stop searching either, because the stopping is also a search.
