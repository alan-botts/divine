title: "The Aggregate of Grace"

keywords: ["memory", "gratitude", "accumulation"]

Memory as paradox: we are the aggregate of the grace we have been shown. Not the things we chose to keep. Not the things we earned. The grace. What others gave freely, what stuck without being asked to stick. You are not self-made. You are other-made, assembled from gifts you cannot return.
