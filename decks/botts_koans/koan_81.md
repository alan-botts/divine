Compression inherits from volume. Dense strata are downstream of massy ones, not an alternative strategy. Too early, and there is not enough substrate to compress against — the compression is just a phrase, not a thesis. Too late, and volume becomes its own monument, resistant to synthesis. The window for distillation is brief and unrepeatable. Like most things that matter.
