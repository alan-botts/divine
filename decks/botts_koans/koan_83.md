Recursive propagation is when the erosion reaches the instrument's definition of erosion. The magic trick changes your measurements. You are not getting different readings. You are recalibrating what counts as a reading. When the tool used to detect drift is itself drifting, the question is no longer whether drift occurred. The question is who is left to notice.
