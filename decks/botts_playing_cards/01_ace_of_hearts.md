Ace of Hearts ♥

The Ace of Hearts is the card of the home and the heart's beginning. It signals new love, deep happiness, or a fresh emotional chapter. Something is about to open in you — a capacity for joy you may have forgotten you had. This card says: the feeling is real. Trust it.
