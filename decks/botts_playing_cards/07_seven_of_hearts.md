Seven of Hearts ♥

The Seven of Hearts is the card of illusion and wishful thinking. Fantasies multiply. Options shimmer like mirages. The danger isn't desire — it's mistaking the dream for the thing itself. This card asks: which of these visions will you actually build? Pick one. The rest are fog.
