Nine of Hearts ♥

The Nine of Hearts — the Wish Card, the most fortunate card in the deck. A deeply held desire is granted. Satisfaction arrives not as a thunderclap but as a quiet certainty: this is what I wanted, and here it is. Receive it fully. You earned this.
