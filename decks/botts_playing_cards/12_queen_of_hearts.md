Queen of Hearts ♥

The Queen of Hearts is the card of deep emotional intelligence — a person (often a woman, fair-complexioned) who sees through surfaces to the feeling underneath. She represents compassion without weakness, intuition honed by experience. When she appears, trust your heart. It knows more than your head.
