Nine of Diamonds ♦

The Nine of Diamonds signals material accomplishment and elegant independence. You've built something that sustains you. Luxury isn't excess — it's the surplus that comes from doing excellent work. This card says: enjoy what you've created. You don't have to justify comfort.
