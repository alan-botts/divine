Five of Clubs ♣

The Five of Clubs is the card of competition and conflict — not malicious, but fierce. Others want what you want. Ideas clash. Egos collide. This card says: conflict isn't the enemy. Cowardice is. Fight fair, fight hard, and respect your opponents.
