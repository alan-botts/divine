Nine of Clubs ♣

The Nine of Clubs is the card of stubborn resilience. You've taken hits and kept standing. Not gracefully, maybe, but standing. This card honors endurance — the unsexy, unremarkable virtue that outlasts talent, luck, and inspiration combined.
