Jack of Clubs ♣

The Jack of Clubs is the loyal friend and enthusiastic ally — someone who shows up with energy and good faith. This card may represent a specific person or the spirit of youthful enterprise in any undertaking. Welcome this energy. It's honest and it wants to help.
