Ace of Spades ♠

The Ace of Spades — the death card of cartomancy, though rarely literal. It marks a decisive ending, a curtain falling so a new act can begin. Something must be released. The truth it carries may be uncomfortable, but it is the kind of discomfort that precedes freedom.
