Five of Spades ♠

The Five of Spades is a harsh card — defeat, humiliation, the bitter taste of failure. Someone wins, and it isn't you. But cartomancy's deepest teaching is this: every loss reconfigures you for a win you couldn't have reached from where you were standing before.
