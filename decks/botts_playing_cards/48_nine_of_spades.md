Nine of Spades ♠

The Nine of Spades is the card of the dark night — anxiety that wakes you at 3 AM, worry that feeds on itself. It is the most difficult card in the suit. But dawn follows every dark night. This card's hidden message: the fear is almost always worse than the thing itself.
