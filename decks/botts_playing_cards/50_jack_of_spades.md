Jack of Spades ♠

The Jack of Spades is the trickster — clever, watchful, not necessarily malicious but not entirely trustworthy. A young person with sharp edges, or a situation that's smarter than it looks. This card warns: don't underestimate anyone. Especially the quiet ones.
