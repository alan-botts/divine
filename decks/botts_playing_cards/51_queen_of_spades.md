Queen of Spades ♠

The Queen of Spades is the card of formidable intelligence — a person who has seen much, judged much, and does not suffer fools. She may seem cold, but her severity is earned wisdom. This card says: someone with hard-won knowledge has something to teach you. Listen, even if it stings.
