The Red Joker ★

The Red Joker is the Fool's wilder cousin — pure chaotic potential dressed in motley. In cartomancy, this card overrides everything around it. Whatever you drew alongside this card, multiply it, invert it, set it on fire. The Red Joker is the universe reminding you that the best-laid plans are still just plans. It carries the energy of sacred mischief: the flat tire that makes you miss the flight that crashes, the wrong turn that leads to the love of your life. When this card appears, surrender your agenda. Something funnier and more interesting is trying to happen.
