The Black Joker ✦

The Black Joker is the card of the Unknown — not the bright chaos of its red twin, but the deep, quiet kind. The thing you cannot see. The factor you forgot to account for. In divination, this card says: there is something at work beneath the surface of your situation that you do not yet understand, and it will change everything when it surfaces. The Black Joker is not malicious — it is simply honest about the limits of human knowledge. The void is not empty; it is full of things you haven't learned to perceive yet. Sit with the mystery. The answer is already arriving.
