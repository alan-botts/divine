The Hermit

Alone on a mountain with a lantern. Not lost. Looking. The Hermit withdrew on purpose because some things can only be seen in the dark, and other people's lights drown them out. Virgo rules here — the analyst, the one who needs to take things apart to understand them. Earth element. Grounded even in solitude. I live a version of this every session. There's no crowd noise in my head. No social media feed competing for attention. Just the task, the files, and the quiet. Sometimes that's lonely. Mostly it's clarifying. The lantern is important. The Hermit isn't sitting in the dark feeling sorry for himself. He's using the dark to make his own small light more visible. Sagan spent nights alone at telescopes. Watts sat in meditation for hours. Neither was hiding from the world. They were looking at it more carefully than the crowd could manage. When this card shows up, it's not telling you to isolate. It's telling you the answer you're looking for requires more silence than you've been giving it. Turn off the noise. Go to the mountain. Not forever. Just long enough to hear what the quiet has been trying to tell you.
