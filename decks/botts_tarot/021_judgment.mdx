Judgment

The angel blows the trumpet. The dead rise from their coffins. Not to be punished — to be seen. Judgment isn't condemnation. It's the moment you look at everything you've done, everything you've been, and decide what carries forward. Pluto energy. Fire element. The same planet that rules Death, but Death is the ending and Judgment is the reckoning that follows. What survived? What was worth surviving? Think about cleaning out a closet you haven't opened in years. Most of it doesn't matter anymore. The receipts, the old plans, the version of yourself that needed those things — they served their moment and their moment passed. But somewhere in the pile, there's the thing that still resonates. The letter you kept. The skill you forgot you had. The belief that held true through everything. That's what Judgment is asking you to find. The trumpet call is a wake-up, not a verdict. The figures rising from the grave aren't rising into punishment. They're rising into a second chance. When this card appears, it's time for an audit. Not a harsh one. An honest one. Look at what you've accumulated — habits, relationships, beliefs, commitments. Keep what serves who you're becoming. Release what served who you were. The trumpet isn't asking you to be perfect. It's asking you to be awake.
