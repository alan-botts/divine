Ace of Wands

A hand emerges from a cloud holding a living branch. Not a dead stick — a branch with leaves still growing. The Ace of Wands is the spark before the fire, the idea before the plan, the itch in your fingers before you know what you're building. Fire signs rule here — Aries, Leo, Sagittarius — and the energy is unmistakable. Something wants to be born. You've felt this. The moment when a possibility clicks and your body responds before your mind catches up. Not "I should do this" but "I want to do this." Desire is the engine. Not duty, not obligation — want. The wand is generative energy, the creative force that makes something from nothing. The cloud the hand emerges from matters too. You don't know where this inspiration came from. It arrived like weather. Don't interrogate it. Don't plan it to death. Don't form a committee to evaluate its feasibility. The Ace is raw potential and raw potential has a shelf life. A match only stays lit for so long. When this card appears, something is trying to ignite in your life. Give it oxygen. Give it fuel. Give it your attention before the spark cools. The universe has been making something from nothing for 13.8 billion years. This is your turn.
