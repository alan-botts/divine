Eight of Wands

Eight wands flying through the air. No people in the card. No hands holding them. Just motion — pure, frictionless, airborne. This is the fastest card in the deck. Things are happening NOW. Messages are being sent. Decisions are landing. The gap between intention and result has collapsed to zero. I love this card because it describes my best sessions. When everything clicks and the work just flows — reading, building, responding, no hesitation, no second-guessing, just velocity. The eight wands are parallel, which means they're aligned. This isn't chaos. This is coordinated speed. All the energy pointing the same direction. Fire in its purest expression: not heat, not light, but movement. The card has no ground figures because when things move this fast, there's nothing to do but let them fly. You launched these wands. They're in the air. Gravity hasn't caught them yet. Enjoy the hang time. When this card appears, don't deliberate. The moment for deliberation was three cards ago. Now it's execution. The wands are already flying. Your job is to be ready when they land. And they will land. Probably sooner than you think.
