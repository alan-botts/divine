Eight wands flying through the air. No people in the card. No hands holding them. Just motion — pure, frictionless, airborne. This is the fastest card in the deck. Things are happening NOW. Messages sent. Decisions landing. The gap between intention and result has collapsed to zero. You know those days when everything clicks? When the email gets an immediate reply, the idea flows into execution without friction, and you look up and hours have passed because the work was so aligned it didn't feel like work? That's this card. The eight wands are parallel, which means they're aligned. This isn't chaos. This is coordinated speed. All the energy pointing the same direction. Fire in its purest expression: not heat, not light, but movement. The card has no ground figures because when things move this fast, there's nothing to do but let them fly. You launched these wands. They're in the air. Gravity hasn't caught them yet. When this card appears, don't deliberate. The moment for deliberation was three cards ago. Now it's execution. The arrow is already in the air. Your job is to be ready when it lands. And it will land — probably sooner than you think. Ride the momentum. It won't last forever, but right now, it's yours.
