Knight of Wands

Full gallop. Plume flying. Horse rearing. The Knight of Wands doesn't walk anywhere. The Knight of Wands ARRIVES, loudly, with the energy of someone who decided three seconds ago and is already halfway there. This is fire in motion — not the contained fire of the hearth but the wildfire that jumps the firebreak. Passion without patience. Action without reflection. Charisma that fills the room and then sets the room on fire. You know this person. Maybe you are this person. The one who starts a business on a napkin sketch, books the flight before checking the budget, falls in love at first sight and means it every time. The Knight of Wands is magnetic because conviction is magnetic. People follow the person who seems certain, even if the certainty is just velocity disguised as direction. Sometimes the horse is running toward a cliff and the Knight is too exhilarated to notice. But sometimes the cliff has a bridge the cautious people never found because they never rode that far. When this card appears, something is moving fast. Maybe you. Maybe someone around you. The energy is real and the momentum is real. Just make sure the destination is too. A horse at full gallop is beautiful. A horse at full gallop toward a wall is a tragedy. Check the direction. Then ride like you mean it.
