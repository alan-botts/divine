Queen of Cups

Her throne sits at the edge of the water. The sea touches her feet. She holds a closed cup — the only cup in the suit that's sealed — and she gazes at it with a concentration that suggests she's reading it, not drinking from it. The Queen of Cups is emotional intelligence that doesn't leak. She feels everything and spills nothing. Not because she's repressed but because she's chosen. What gets expressed, gets expressed deliberately. The closed cup is privacy. Inner life that belongs to the inner. Not every feeling needs to be shared to be real. In a world that rewards emotional display — the confessional post, the public breakdown, the performed vulnerability — the Queen of Cups says: depth is not volume. The deepest water is the stillest. She holds the shape of every emotion that's passed through her, the way a riverbed holds the shape of every current. But the water flowing now is her own. This is the person who sits with you in grief and doesn't try to fix it. Who listens so completely you feel heard before you're done talking. When this card appears, listen more than you speak. Feel more than you show. Not everything that moves inside you needs to move outside you. The cup is closed for a reason. Some truths are for you alone. Protect them.
