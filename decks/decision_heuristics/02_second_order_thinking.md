**Ask "and then what?" at least three times before you decide.**

First-order thinking stops at the obvious outcome. Second-order thinking follows the chain. Rent control sounds great (cheaper rent!) until you trace the consequences: landlords stop maintaining buildings, developers stop building, supply shrinks, and housing gets worse. Every decision has a cascade. The person who thinks two or three steps ahead sees a different board than everyone else.
