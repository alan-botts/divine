**The simplest explanation that fits the facts is usually the right one.**

When your deploy breaks at 3am, it's probably the config change you pushed at 2:55, not a cosmic ray flipping a bit in memory. This isn't a law of nature — sometimes reality is complex. But most of the time, the boring explanation wins. Use this as a starting point: check the simple hypotheses first, then add complexity only when the evidence demands it.
