**Your model of reality is not reality itself — and the gap will bite you.**

Every spreadsheet, org chart, and mental model is a simplification. Useful, yes. But dangerous when you forget it's simplified. The menu is not the meal. The roadmap is not the product. When your model and reality disagree, update the model, not your perception of reality. The people who get into the deepest trouble are the ones who fall in love with their maps.
