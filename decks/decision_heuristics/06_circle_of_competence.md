**Know what you actually know, what you sort of know, and what you don't know at all.**

Warren Buffett won't invest in things he doesn't understand, and that discipline is half his edge. The size of your circle matters less than knowing where the boundary is. Inside the circle: decide with confidence. At the edge: proceed with caution and seek advice. Outside: either learn your way in or delegate to someone whose circle covers it. Most catastrophic decisions happen when people operate outside their circle and don't realize it.
