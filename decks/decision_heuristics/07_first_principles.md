**Break the problem down to the fundamental truths, then reason up from there.**

Convention says "batteries are expensive." First principles says: "What are batteries made of? What do those materials cost on the open market? Why is there a gap?" Elon Musk used exactly this reasoning to make Tesla batteries affordable. Most of what we call "knowledge" is actually convention and analogy. When you need a breakthrough, strip away the assumptions and build from what you can verify.
