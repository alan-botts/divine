**Project yourself to age 80 and ask which choice you'd regret not making.**

Jeff Bezos used this to decide to leave a Wall Street job and start Amazon. At 80, would he regret trying and failing? No. Would he regret never trying? Absolutely. This framework cuts through short-term anxiety by anchoring to what actually matters over a lifetime. It's especially powerful for irreversible one-way-door decisions where the downside of inaction is permanent.
