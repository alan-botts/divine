**Before evaluating any specific case, check how often this type of thing usually happens.**

Your startup idea feels special. But what's the base rate for startups succeeding? About 10%. That doesn't mean you'll fail — it means you need to understand what makes you different from the 90%. People consistently ignore base rates in favor of narrative. The doctor says the test is 99% accurate, but if only 1 in 10,000 people have the disease, a positive result is still probably a false positive. Always ask: "How often does this happen in general?"
