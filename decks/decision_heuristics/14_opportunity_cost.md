**The true cost of anything is what you give up to get it.**

Every hour spent in a meeting is an hour not spent building. Every dollar invested in Project A is a dollar not invested in Project B. People fixate on the direct cost of a decision and forget the invisible cost: the best alternative they didn't pursue. When you say yes to something, you're implicitly saying no to everything else you could have done with those resources. Make that tradeoff explicit.
