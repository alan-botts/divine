---
title: "Sunk Cost Fallacy"
number: 15
keywords: ["sunk cost", "past investment", "letting go"]
asset_url: ""
---

**Money, time, or effort already spent should not factor into decisions about the future.**

"But we've already invested six months in this!" So what? The question is: going forward, is this the best use of the next six months? Past investment is gone regardless of what you do now. The sunk cost fallacy keeps people in bad jobs, bad relationships, and bad projects because walking away feels like "wasting" what came before. It isn't waste — it's tuition. Learn from it and move on.
