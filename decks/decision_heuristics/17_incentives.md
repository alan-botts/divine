**Never ask why someone is doing something until you understand what they're rewarded for doing.**

Charlie Munger called it the most important mental model of all: "Show me the incentive and I'll show you the outcome." People respond to incentives — financial, social, emotional — often in ways they don't consciously recognize. If a salesperson pushes a product you don't need, it's probably because their commission structure rewards it. When behavior puzzles you, follow the incentives. They explain almost everything.
