**Concentrate overwhelming force at the decisive point.**

German military doctrine held that every plan needs a Schwerpunkt — the one thing that everything else supports. Not three priorities. Not five. One. This applies everywhere: if your startup has seven strategic initiatives, you have zero. Pick the one that matters most, resource it fully, and subordinate everything else to it. Dispersion is the enemy of impact. The question is always: "What is the main effort, and is everything else aligned to support it?"
