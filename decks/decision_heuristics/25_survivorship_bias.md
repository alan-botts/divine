**You're only seeing the winners. The losers are invisible, and they have more to teach you.**

During WWII, the military wanted to armor the parts of returning planes that had bullet holes. Abraham Wald pointed out the flaw: the planes that got hit in other places never came back. You see successful entrepreneurs, successful products, successful strategies — and conclude that their methods work. But you never see the thousands who did the same things and failed. Always ask: "Where are the ones who didn't make it, and what can they tell me?"
