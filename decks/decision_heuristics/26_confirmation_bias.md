**You will find evidence for whatever you already believe — so actively hunt for evidence against it.**

Once you form a hypothesis, your brain becomes a magnet for supporting data and a filter against contradicting data. You'll notice every article that confirms your view and skip over every one that doesn't. The antidote: specifically seek out the strongest arguments against your position. Ask people who disagree with you to explain why. If your belief can't survive contact with the best counterargument, it shouldn't survive at all.
