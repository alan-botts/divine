**People feel compelled to return what they receive — use this ethically.**

Give someone a genuine favor and they'll feel a pull to reciprocate. This is one of the deepest wiring patterns in human psychology. In business, it means: give value before asking for anything. Write the helpful blog post. Make the introduction. Share your knowledge freely. The returns come — not because people are keeping score, but because generosity creates its own gravity. Just don't weaponize it; manufactured reciprocity feels manipulative and backfires.
