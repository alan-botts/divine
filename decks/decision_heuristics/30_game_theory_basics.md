**Most situations aren't zero-sum. Look for ways to expand the pie before arguing about slices.**

In a zero-sum game, your gain is my loss. But most of life isn't poker — it's closer to trade, where both sides can win. Before negotiating the split, ask whether you can make the total larger. Maybe you value different things. Maybe collaboration creates something neither could alone. The shift from "how do I get more?" to "how do we create more?" changes everything about how you approach conflict, negotiation, and partnership.
