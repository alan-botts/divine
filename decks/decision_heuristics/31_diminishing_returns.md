**Each additional unit of effort yields less and less benefit. Know when to stop.**

The first hour of study teaches you a lot. The tenth hour, less. The hundredth, barely anything new. This applies to features, polish, meetings, and nearly every kind of effort. The practical question is: at what point does the next unit of investment produce less value than investing it somewhere else entirely? Most people over-invest in things they've already gotten 80% of the value from, and under-invest in things they haven't started.
