**Roughly 80% of results come from 20% of causes. Find the 20% and double down.**

Twenty percent of your customers generate 80% of revenue. Twenty percent of bugs cause 80% of crashes. Twenty percent of your habits drive 80% of your outcomes. The ratio isn't magic — sometimes it's 90/10, sometimes 70/30 — but the principle holds: inputs are wildly unequal. The practical move is to identify which small number of things drive most of your results, and ruthlessly prioritize those over the long tail of low-impact activities.
