**A chain of factors where any one can go to zero makes the entire result zero.**

Your product can be brilliant, your marketing flawless, your team stellar — but if nobody can figure out the signup flow, you get zero customers. In any multiplicative system, the weakest link doesn't just reduce the outcome; if it drops to zero, it eliminates it entirely. Before you optimize your strengths, make sure none of your critical factors are at or near zero. Fix the fatal flaw before polishing the crown jewel.
