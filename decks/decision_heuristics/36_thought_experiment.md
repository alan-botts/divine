**Run the scenario in your head before you run it in the world.**

Einstein discovered relativity by imagining himself riding a beam of light. You don't need to be Einstein to use this: "What would happen if we 10x'd the price? What if this competitor didn't exist? What if we had to launch in two weeks instead of two months?" Thought experiments let you stress-test ideas without spending resources. They reveal assumptions you didn't know you had. The cost is five minutes of genuine imagination.
