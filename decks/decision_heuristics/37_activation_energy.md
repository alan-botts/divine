**Getting started requires more energy than keeping going. Design for easier starts.**

A chemical reaction needs a spark. A habit needs a cue. A project needs someone to write the first line. The hardest part is almost always initiation, not continuation. So reduce the activation energy: lay out your gym clothes the night before, open the document and type one sentence, schedule the meeting instead of thinking about scheduling it. Lower the barrier to starting and momentum handles the rest.
