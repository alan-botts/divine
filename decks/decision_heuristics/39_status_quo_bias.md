**People disproportionately prefer the current state of affairs, even when alternatives are objectively better.**

Organ donation rates are dramatically higher in countries where the default is opt-in versus opt-out — even though the decision is equally important. Defaults are powerful because changing requires effort, and status quo feels safe. In product design, in policy, in your own life: examine what you're keeping simply because it's what you already have. Sometimes the best decision is to actively re-choose rather than passively continue.
