**Critical systems need backup systems. The cost of redundancy is always less than the cost of total failure.**

Airplanes have multiple engines, multiple hydraulic systems, multiple communication systems. Not because engineers expect all of them to fail, but because the consequence of failure is catastrophic. Apply this proportionally: the more severe the downside, the more backup you need. Two copies of important data. A second supplier for critical materials. A plan B for the launch. Redundancy feels wasteful until the moment it saves you.
