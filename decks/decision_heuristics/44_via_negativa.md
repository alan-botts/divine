**Sometimes the best intervention is removing something rather than adding something.**

Struggling with productivity? Before adding a new app or system, ask what you should stop doing. Product feels bloated? Don't add features — remove the ones nobody uses. Feeling overwhelmed? Don't add a meditation practice — cancel three commitments. We're biased toward addition, but subtraction is often more powerful and always cheaper. Michelangelo said he just removed everything that wasn't David. Often the path forward is through less, not more.
