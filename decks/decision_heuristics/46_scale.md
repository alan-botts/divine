**What works at one scale often breaks at another. Always ask whether your current approach survives a 10x change.**

A process that works for a team of five collapses at fifty. A database that handles a thousand queries chokes at a million. A friendship maintained through weekly dinners doesn't scale to five hundred friends. Scale isn't just "more of the same" — it introduces qualitatively different problems. When planning, ask: "Will this approach still work if demand doubles? If it 10x's?" If not, you're building on a foundation that will crack under its own growth.
