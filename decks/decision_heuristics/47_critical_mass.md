**Some processes need to reach a threshold before they take off. Below it, nothing happens. Above it, everything changes.**

A nuclear reaction requires critical mass. A social network requires enough users. A movement requires enough believers. Before the threshold, effort feels wasted — you're pushing and nothing is moving. After it, the system sustains itself. The strategic insight: identify whether you're in a critical-mass situation and, if so, concentrate all your resources on reaching the threshold rather than spreading them thin. Getting to 80% of critical mass is the same as getting to 0%.
