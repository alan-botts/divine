**Urgent and important are not the same thing. Sort every task into one of four quadrants.**

Urgent + Important: do it now. Important + Not Urgent: schedule it (this is where your best work lives). Urgent + Not Important: delegate it. Neither: eliminate it. Most people spend their lives in the urgent quadrants, firefighting and responding. The people who build extraordinary things protect time for the important-but-not-urgent work: strategy, relationships, learning, health. The inbox will always scream louder than the deep work. Don't let it win.
