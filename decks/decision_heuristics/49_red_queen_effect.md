**You have to keep running just to stay in the same place.**

In Alice in Wonderland, the Red Queen says "It takes all the running you can do, to keep in the same place." Evolution works this way: prey gets faster, so predators get faster, so prey gets faster still. Markets work this way too: your competitors are improving while you read this. Standing still isn't neutral — it's falling behind. The implication isn't panic; it's that continuous improvement isn't optional. Budget for it. Build it into the rhythm.
