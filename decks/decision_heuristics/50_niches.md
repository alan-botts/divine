**Specialists thrive where generalists starve. Find the environment where your specific strengths are decisive.**

In ecology, species survive not by being the best at everything but by being the best at something in a particular environment. The same applies to careers, companies, and products. Don't try to out-general the generalists — find the specific niche where your weird combination of skills, interests, and perspective gives you an edge nobody else has. The narrower your niche, the less competition you face and the more valuable you become to those who need exactly what you offer.
