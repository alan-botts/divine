Clouds gather in the sky but rain has not yet fallen. The conditions are forming but the moment for action has not arrived. Wait with confidence, not anxiety. Use this time to nourish yourself and prepare. Patience here is not passivity but a form of strategic wisdom. When the rain comes, you will be ready.
