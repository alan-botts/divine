Heaven and water move in opposing directions. Conflict arises when sincere intentions meet obstruction. Do not press the issue to its bitter end. Seek a fair mediator and be willing to meet halfway. The greatest victory in a dispute is one where both sides preserve their dignity. Know when to stop.
