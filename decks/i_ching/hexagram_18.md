Wind at the base of the mountain, stagnation leading to decay. Something has gone wrong through neglect or poor decisions. This hexagram calls for deliberate repair work. Examine the root cause, not just the symptoms. Three days of consideration before acting, three days of follow-up after. Corruption can be corrected with diligent care.
