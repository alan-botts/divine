Thunder and lightning together, illuminating and shaking at once. An obstacle blocks the path and must be dealt with decisively. This is not the time for half measures. Bite through the obstruction clearly and firmly. Justice applied fairly restores order. Be direct, be honest, and do not let sentiment prevent necessary action.
