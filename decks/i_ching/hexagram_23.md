=========

The mountain rests on a thin foundation of earth. What once stood firm is eroding away. This hexagram warns that the current structure cannot be saved. Do not fight the decline. Accept the loss gracefully and wait for the natural cycle to bring renewal. What falls apart now will create the ground for something new.
