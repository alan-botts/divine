Fire upon fire, light doubled. Fire clings to fuel to exist, just as clarity depends on something real to illuminate. This hexagram counsels brightness and awareness. See things as they are, understand your dependencies, and shine your light without burning recklessly. Sustained brilliance requires a steady supply of genuine fuel.
