The lake rests atop the mountain, moisture and stillness in exchange. Genuine influence works through openness and receptivity, not force. This hexagram describes the natural attraction that arises when you are truly open to another person or situation. Empty yourself of preconceptions. Mutual influence begins with genuine willingness to be affected.
