The sun rises over the earth, illuminating everything gradually. Progress comes naturally and visibly. Your efforts are being recognized and rewarded. This is a time of steady advancement. Accept the recognition graciously but remain committed to serving the greater good. The rising sun illuminates not to glorify itself but to benefit everything it touches.
