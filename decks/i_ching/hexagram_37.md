Wind emerges from fire, warmth creating movement. The family, and by extension any close group, functions well when each member fulfills their role with sincerity. This hexagram emphasizes that outer harmony begins with inner order. Set your own house in order before attempting to influence the wider world. Words must be backed by consistent action.
