Fire above the lake, elements that naturally diverge. People or forces are moving in different directions. Yet opposition does not preclude cooperation. This hexagram advises finding common ground without erasing differences. The creative tension between opposites can produce something neither side could achieve alone. Seek understanding, not uniformity.
