The lake at the foot of the mountain, its waters feeding the heights. Decrease in one area brings increase in another. This hexagram counsels voluntary simplification. Reduce excess, eliminate the unnecessary, and direct your resources toward what matters most. Sincere sacrifice, even of small things, generates disproportionate returns when offered with genuine intent.
