Wind and thunder together, each amplifying the other. This is a time of genuine increase. Seize the opportunity to undertake great things. When you see a fault in yourself, correct it. When you see goodness in others, emulate it. Generosity during times of increase creates bonds that sustain you during future times of decrease.
