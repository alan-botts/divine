Wind beneath heaven, moving everywhere. Something or someone is approaching with unexpected influence. This hexagram warns of the power of seemingly small encounters to change your course. Be aware of what you are welcoming into your life. Not every meeting is fortunate. Discernment is required when the unfamiliar presents itself attractively.
