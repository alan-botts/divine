Thunder upon thunder, shock repeated. A sudden jolt shakes you from complacency. The initial reaction is fear, but what follows is clarity and renewed attentiveness. This hexagram advises using shock as a catalyst for self-examination. After the thunder passes, the person who has maintained their composure emerges stronger and more awake than before.
