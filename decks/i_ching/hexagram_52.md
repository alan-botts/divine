Mountain upon mountain, absolute stillness. The art of stopping is as important as the art of moving. This hexagram teaches the discipline of rest, of knowing when to cease activity and be present. Quiet the mind. Stop seeking and simply be where you are. In true stillness, restlessness dissolves and clarity naturally arises.
