Fire on the mountain, bright but without a permanent home. The wanderer must conduct themselves with special care, relying on alertness and good manners rather than established position. This hexagram advises being mindful of your status as a guest or newcomer. Small attentive actions build trust. Do not overreach when you are on unfamiliar ground.
