Wind upon wind, gentle but ceaseless. The most enduring influence works not through dramatic force but through steady, persistent presence, like wind that shapes stone over centuries. This hexagram advises gentle persistence. State your intention clearly and maintain it consistently. The effect may be invisible day to day, but over time, it transforms everything.
