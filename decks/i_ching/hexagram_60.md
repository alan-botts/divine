Water above the lake, filling to a natural boundary. Limitations are not restrictions but the natural structures that give form to life. This hexagram counsels setting reasonable boundaries for yourself and others. Excessive limitation becomes oppression; no limitation becomes chaos. Find the constraints that enable rather than diminish, and accept them willingly.
