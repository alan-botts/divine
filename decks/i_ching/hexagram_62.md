Thunder above the mountain, a small bird in flight. This is a time for attending to small matters rather than attempting grand projects. Exceed expectations in minor things. Be more careful than usual, more humble than necessary, more frugal than required. The bird that flies too high meets the storm. Stay close to earth and tend to what is near.
