Water over fire, each element in its proper place. Everything is in order, the task is complete. But this is precisely the moment of greatest danger, for the natural tendency after achievement is complacency. Pay attention to small disorders before they grow. The beginning was easy; maintaining what has been accomplished requires constant vigilance.
