The Fool stands at the edge of a cliff, pack slung over one shoulder, gazing upward rather than at the ground beneath. This card marks the start of a journey taken on pure faith. It suggests a willingness to leap without knowing where you will land, trusting that the fall itself is part of the path. When The Fool appears, consider what new adventure is calling you, and whether your hesitation is wisdom or merely fear.
