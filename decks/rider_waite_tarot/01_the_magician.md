The Magician stands with one hand raised to the sky and the other pointing to the earth, channeling power between realms. All four elemental tools lie before him on the table. This card signals that you have everything you need to accomplish your goal. The question is not whether you have the resources, but whether you will focus your intention and act. Scattered energy produces nothing; directed will transforms everything.
