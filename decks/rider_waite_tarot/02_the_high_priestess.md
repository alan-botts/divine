The High Priestess sits between two pillars, a veil of pomegranates behind her, holding a scroll of hidden law. She represents the things you know but cannot yet articulate, the wisdom that lives beneath rational thought. When she appears, stop seeking external answers. The information you need is already within you, waiting for stillness and attention. Listen to what surfaces in quiet moments.
