The Empress sits in a lush garden, crowned with stars, surrounded by growing things. She embodies creative abundance and the patient nurturing that brings ideas to fruition. This card asks you to tend what you have planted. Growth takes time, but the conditions are favorable. Create an environment where things can flourish rather than forcing outcomes. Comfort, beauty, and care are not luxuries but necessities for sustained creation.
