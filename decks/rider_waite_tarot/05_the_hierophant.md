The Hierophant sits between two pillars, raising his hand in blessing, two acolytes kneeling before him. He represents established wisdom, the knowledge passed down through systems and mentors. This card suggests seeking guidance from those who have walked the path before you. Sometimes the conventional approach exists for good reason. Before you reinvent, understand what has already been learned.
