An old figure stands alone on a mountain peak, holding a lantern that illuminates only the next step. The Hermit withdraws not to escape but to find clarity that crowds obscure. This card invites you to step away from noise and opinion. The answer you are seeking requires solitude and honest self-examination. Sometimes you must walk alone for a while to find the path that is truly yours.
