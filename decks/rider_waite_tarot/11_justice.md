A figure sits with sword raised in one hand and balanced scales in the other. Justice represents the natural consequences of past actions arriving in the present. This is not punishment but clarity: every choice has led here. When Justice appears, be honest about your role in your current situation. Take responsibility where it is due, and trust that fair outcomes are possible when truth is acknowledged.
