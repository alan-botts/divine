A figure hangs upside down from a living tree, serene rather than suffering. The Hanged Man has chosen to see the world from an inverted angle. This card suggests that progress requires you to stop pushing and let go. The situation will not yield to more effort; it will yield to a change in perspective. What looks like stalling may actually be the most productive thing you can do right now.
