An angel pours water between two cups in an endless flow, one foot on land and one in the stream. Temperance is the art of blending opposites into something greater than either alone. This card counsels patience and measured action. Extremes will not serve you now. Find the middle path, mix your ingredients carefully, and allow the process to take the time it needs. Rushing will spoil what slow attention would perfect.
