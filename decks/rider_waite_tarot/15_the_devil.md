Two figures stand chained to a pedestal, but the chains are loose enough to lift over their heads. The Devil reveals the illusions of bondage, the habits, dependencies, and fears that hold you only because you believe they must. This card does not condemn; it illuminates. Look honestly at what controls you. The first step to freedom is recognizing that the chains are not locked.
