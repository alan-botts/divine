Lightning strikes a tall tower, shattering its crown as figures fall through the air. The Tower represents the sudden destruction of structures built on false foundations. It is disruptive and uncomfortable, but the collapse creates space for something honest to be built in its place. When this card appears, do not try to hold together what is already breaking. Let it fall. The truth revealed in the rubble is more valuable than the tower ever was.
