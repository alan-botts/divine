Political disorders are like a wasting disease: easy to cure in the early stages when symptoms are hard to detect, but once the symptoms become obvious, the disease is nearly incurable. The prince who waits for trouble to arrive has already lost the advantage of time.

— Chapter III: Mixed Principalities
