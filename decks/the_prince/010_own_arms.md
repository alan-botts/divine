Mercenaries are useless and dangerous: they are undisciplined, ambitious, and brave only among friends. A prince who relies on hired soldiers is never secure. The foundation of all states is good arms, and good arms can only be your own — troops with a personal stake in your survival.

— Chapter XII-XIII: On Mercenaries and Auxiliary Troops
