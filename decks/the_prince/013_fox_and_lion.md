A prince must know how to act as both a fox and a lion. The lion cannot protect himself from traps, and the fox cannot protect himself from wolves. One who relies solely on force will be outwitted; one who relies solely on cunning will be overpowered. Master both natures.

— Chapter XVIII: In What Way Princes Should Keep Faith
