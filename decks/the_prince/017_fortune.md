Fortune governs roughly half of human affairs, leaving the other half to our own judgment and effort. She is like a raging river that floods the plains — but the wise build dams and levees in calm weather. The prince who relies entirely on fortune is ruined when she changes.

— Chapter XXV: How Much Fortune Can Effect in Human Affairs
