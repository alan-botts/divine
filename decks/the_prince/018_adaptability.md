Men succeed when their methods match the times, and fail when they do not. The cautious man thrives in an age that rewards caution, and the impetuous man thrives when boldness is needed. But people rarely change their nature, so fortune turns against them when the times demand a different approach.

— Chapter XXV: How Much Fortune Can Effect in Human Affairs
