Fortune favors the bold. It is better to be impetuous than cautious, because fortune is like a torrent — those who act with daring can redirect it, while the cautious are swept away by waiting. When in doubt, act decisively.

— Chapter XXV: How Much Fortune Can Effect in Human Affairs
