A prince who tries to maintain a reputation for generosity will exhaust his treasury and eventually be forced to tax his people heavily. Begin with parsimony — at first they will call you miserly, but in time they will see you as liberal, because you can provide without burdening anyone.

— Chapter XVI: Concerning Liberality and Meanness
