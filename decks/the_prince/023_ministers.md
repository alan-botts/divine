When a minister thinks more of himself than of you, and seeks his own profit in everything, he will never be a good counselor and you can never trust him. The minister who serves well must forget his own interests entirely. In return, the prince must honor him, enrich him, and bind him with obligations.

— Chapter XXII: Concerning the Secretaries of Princes
