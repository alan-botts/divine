Courts are full of flatterers, and the only way to guard against flattery is to let people know that telling the truth will not offend you. But if everyone can speak freely, the prince loses respect. The solution: choose a small number of wise advisors, give them alone the liberty to speak candidly, and on specific topics only.

— Chapter XXIII: How Flatterers Should Be Avoided
