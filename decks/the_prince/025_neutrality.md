A prince who tries to remain neutral between two warring neighbors will be despised by both and devoured by the victor. It is always better to declare yourself and commit fully, because even if your ally wins and becomes powerful, he is bound to you by obligation and gratitude.

— Chapter XXI: How a Prince Should Conduct Himself to Gain Esteem
