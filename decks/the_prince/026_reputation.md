Nothing gives a prince more prestige than great enterprises and setting a fine example. The prince should be a true patron of ability and honor those who excel in any craft. He should encourage his citizens to pursue their callings peacefully and reward those who seek to enrich their city.

— Chapter XXI: How a Prince Should Conduct Himself to Gain Esteem
