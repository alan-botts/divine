Auxiliary troops — soldiers lent by a powerful ally — are even more dangerous than mercenaries. If they win, you are their prisoner; if they lose, you are ruined. They come as a unified force loyal to someone else. A wise prince would rather lose with his own forces than win with another's.

— Chapter XIII: Concerning Auxiliaries, Mixed Soldiery, and One's Own
