When you conquer a city that has been accustomed to governing itself, you have three options: ruin it, live in it personally, or let it continue under its own laws while extracting tribute. Of these, ruin is the surest. A free city will always rebel in the name of its old liberty — memories of freedom never fade, no matter how well you treat the people.

— Chapter V: How Cities or Principalities Are to Be Governed
