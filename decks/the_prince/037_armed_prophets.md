All armed prophets have conquered, and all unarmed prophets have failed. The nature of people is fickle — it is easy to persuade them of something, but hard to keep them persuaded. When they no longer believe, you must be ready to make them believe by force. Moses, Cyrus, and Romulus could not have enforced their visions unarmed.

— Chapter VI: New Principalities Acquired by One's Own Arms
